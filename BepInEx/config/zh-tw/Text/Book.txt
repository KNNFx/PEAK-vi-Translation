ALWAYS LEND A HAND!=總是向朋友伸出援手！
	Nothing is more important than sticking together as a team!\n\n	When your hands are empty, reach out to a friend who's mid-climb and help them reach the next ledge safely!=	沒有什麼比團隊團結更重要！\n\n當你的雙手空著時，伸手幫助正在攀爬的朋友，讓他們安全到達下一個落腳點！

RULE 4=規則 4
STAY ALERT, STAY ALIVE!=<size\=45>保持警覺活下去！</size>
	An effective scout keeps an eye on their <color\=#0f7f00>Stamina Bar</color> at all times. If it runs out, you'll be temporarily unable to run, jump, and most importantly, climb. \n\n	Worst case scenario, you could fall off the mountain to your death, which is the worst thing to fall off to!=	一位歷練的童軍會時刻注意他們的<color\=#0f7f00>體力</color>。如果體力耗盡，你將暫時無法跑步、跳躍，最重要的是無法攀爬。\n\n最壞的是從山上墜落致死——最糟糕的墜落方式！

RULE 5=規則 5
LIFE IS PAIN!=生活即痛苦！
	As you make your way through the untamed wilderness, it's inevitable that you'll get some scratches and bumps. \n\n	The more hurt you get, the less <color\=#0f7f00>Stamina</color> you'll have to run and climb, so tend to your injuries as soon as possible with a bandage or a healing herb!=	在未開化的荒野中前行，難免會有些擦傷和碰撞。\n\n受傷越重，能用於跑步和攀爬的<color\=#0f7f00>體力</color>就越少，因此要盡快用繃帶或藥草處理傷口！

RULE 6=規則 6
STAY HUNGRY=<size\=45>保持飢餓\n\n</size>
 \n<size\=30>(ACTUALLY, DON'T!)= \n<size\=40>(不要真的做呀！)
 	Getting hurt isn't the only way for your <color\=#0f7f00>Stamina</color> to go downhill fast. As you climb, you'll find yourself experiencing <color\=#b58200>Hunger</color>, for which 10/10 experts recommend eating food.\n\n	Be cautious of eating plants from the wilderness! They could poison you and really mess up your day. If you have the option, choose a packaged snack from a corporation you trust!=	受傷不是讓你的<color\=#0f7f00>體力</color>迅速下降的唯一方式。在攀爬時，你會感到<color\=#b58200>飢餓</color>，10位專家中有10位建議吃食物。\n\n小心食用野外的植物！它們可能有毒，會嚴重影響你的一天。如果有選擇，挑選你信任的企業生產的包裝零食！

RULE 7=規則 7
DON'T OVERPACK!=不要超載！
	Putting a backpack on is a great way to carry additional items, which can make the difference between life and death!\n\n	HOWEVER, the more <color\=#61380a>Weight</color> you carry, the less stamina you'll have to climb! An experienced scout knows to pack everything they need... and nothing more!=	背上背包是攜帶更多道具的好方法，這可能關乎生死！\n\n然而，你攜帶的<color\=#61380a>重量</color>越多，能用於攀爬的體力就越少！經驗豐富的童軍知道帶上所有必需品……不多不少！

RULE 8=規則 8
KEEP AN EYE OUT!=保持警惕！
	In a survival situation, one of the worst things that can happen is getting separated from the group. \n\n	Maintain constant communication whenever possible, and point out your next route in advance to avoid leaving a friend behind!=	在生存情境中，最糟糕的事情之一就是與團隊失散。\n\n盡可能保持持續聯繫，並提前標記你的下一條路線，以免落下朋友！
